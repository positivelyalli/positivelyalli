Latitude vs Humidity

The graph shows the relationship between city latitude and humidity. There does not seem to be a strong relationship between city latitude and humidity. There are some bands of humidity that you can see.

The next step is to break out the latitudes into two sets. The first set will be for negative latitudes. The second set will be for positive latitudes since there seems to be a different relationship for each set. Further research is needed to account for altitude and other factors.
